About us

Global knowledge, local experts to care your hands…………

AMR Ceylon is an emerging small scale glove manufacturing company based in Sri Lanka. Our biggest strength is the industry experience and expertise knowledge of our team who have worked local & global gloves manufacturing facilities more than 20 years.

Our expertise knowledge endeavors to deliver customized hand care products to our valued partners. Our R & D team is always working to develop customer’s product requirements.
